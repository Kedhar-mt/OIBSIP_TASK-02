Early life and education

  • 1.)Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar. He grew up in a middle-class family in a small town called Purnia, also in Bihar.
  • 2.)After completing his schooling from St. Karen's High School in Patna,he cleared AIEEE rank 7 and got admission in Mechanical Engineering in DEC.
  • 3.)However, Sushant was always passionate about acting and dance, and he decided to pursue a career in the entertainment industry instead of pursuing a traditional engineering job. He moved to Mumbai and began his journey as a television actor before making his debut in Bollywood.

National recognition

  • 1.)Sushant Singh Rajput gained national recognition for his work in the entertainment industry, especially in Bollywood. He made his debut in Bollywood with the film "Kai Po Che!" in 2013, which was critically acclaimed and won several awards.
  • 2.)He also starred in other successful films such as "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story," "PK," "Kedarnath," and "Chhichhore." His performances in these films received widespread appreciation, and he won several awards for his acting skills
  • 3.)Apart from his acting skills, Sushant was also a trained dancer and a philanthropist. He was actively involved in various charitable initiatives, such as supporting education for underprivileged children and promoting environmental causes.

Death
